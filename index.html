ENGINEERED FOR TOUGH TERRAIN

Operating from Mt. Austin Heights in Honiara, Alrico Construction Company Limited is a premier civil contractor in the Solomon Islands.

We specialize in delivering high-quality infrastructure projects, from earthworks and road construction to civic market redevelopments and commercial buildings. Partnering with local government and international development initiatives (such as SIIP), we ensure our projects meet rigorous structural and safety standards.
